John Conway's Game Of Life

Controls:

  Click on a cell to toggle its dead/alive state.

  Click Step to advance to the next generation.

  Click Start to increase the generations automatically.

  Click Stop to halt the automatic increase of generations.

  Click Clear to clear the grid of cells.

Rules:

  Empty cells are considered dead, and colored ones are alive.

  Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbours dies.

  Any live cell with two or three live neighbours lives on to the next generation.

  Any live cell with more than three live neighbours dies.

  Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbours becomes a live cell.
